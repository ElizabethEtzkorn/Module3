Wristcutters: A Love Story
















Life is a trip, but the afterlife is one hell of a ride.

After his heart is broken, Zia has had enough of life and decides to end his. When he arrives in the afterlife he makes friends with a few from the same fate. When he learns the love of his living life may be in this melancholy existence, the unlikely friends begin an unlikely search.
